# __2022 CE – 2026 CE__ — War, Sanctions, and Digital Isolation
__Summary:__ Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine in February 2022 triggers unprecedented Western sanctions, mass exodus of global technology platforms, and accelerated construction of a sovereign digital perimeter; the binding constraint is simultaneous loss of foreign hardware, software, and payment infrastructure while attempting to sustain wartime information control and economic function; margin against further isolation is minimal as redundant domestic systems remain incomplete; trajectory is toward deepening digital autarky and internal surveillance intensification.
## __P01. Physical Environment__
__Geography & Spatial Friction:__ The continental East European Plain offers no natural barriers to digital infrastructure deployment, but vast distances between population centers impose latency and maintenance costs on the terrestrial fiber network now cut off from Western peering points.<br><br>
__Climate Regime & Volatility:__ Continental climate unchanged; harsh winters stress data-center cooling economics but impose no novel constraint on digital operations during this window.<br><br>
__Hazard Regime & Exposure:__ No major natural disasters disrupt infrastructure; primary hazards are anthropogenic — cyberattacks, physical sabotage of undersea cables, and wartime targeting of communication nodes.<br><br>
__Resource & Energy Endowment:__ Abundant hydrocarbons and hydroelectric capacity ensure power supply for data centers; rare-earth and semiconductor feedstocks remain accessible via China, though at higher cost and longer lead times than pre-sanctions Western supply chains.<br><br>
__Waste Absorption & Sink Capacity:__ E-waste and heat loads from accelerated domestic server deployment accumulate; sink capacity adequate but recycling infrastructure underdeveloped as Western firms withdraw.<br><br>
## __P02. Biological Environment__
__Pathogen Load & Mortality Baseline:__ COVID-19 pandemic recedes by 2023; endemic respiratory illness and wartime casualty flows elevate mortality but do not directly constrain digital infrastructure operation.<br><br>
__Nutrition & Physiological Vitality:__ Sanctions-driven inflation degrades purchasing power; nutritional impact modest among urban technical workforce but measurable in lower-income regions.<br><br>
__Reproductive Biology & Pairing Dynamics:__ Mobilization and emigration of military-age men disrupt pairing dynamics; no direct impact on digital systems but indirect effect on technical labor availability.<br><br>
__Demographic Renewal & Density:__ Emigration of 500,000–800,000 persons in 2022 alone, disproportionately young, educated, and tech-sector; population density in major cities declines marginally; demographic renewal strained.<br><br>
__Attrition & Dependency Overhang:__ War wounded and families of casualties add to dependency load; pension and welfare systems absorb strain but productive cohort shrinks.<br><br>
## __P03. Economic Environment__
__Subsistence & Provisioning Floor:__ Food security maintained via domestic agriculture; import substitution covers basic goods; subsistence floor holds despite sanctions.<br><br>
__Energy Capture & Conversion:__ Domestic energy abundance insulates economy from fuel shortages; power grid stable; data centers operate without interruption.<br><br>
__Labor Organization & Coercion:__ Mobilization introduces coercive labor extraction; tech workers granted deferrals but uncertainty drives emigration; remaining workforce stretched.<br><br>
__Production & Surplus Capacity:__ Semiconductor imports collapse; domestic chip production decades behind; surplus capacity in digital manufacturing severely constrained; servers, routers, and handsets increasingly scarce.<br><br>
__Storage, Reserves & Buffering:__ Foreign-exchange reserves partially frozen; sovereign wealth fund tapped; buffer against prolonged sanctions eroding.<br><br>
__Exchange, Trade & Logistics:__ SWIFT disconnection for major banks; Mir payment system domestic-only; parallel import channels via third countries add cost and delay; logistics friction elevated.<br><br>
__Value Storage, Credit & Deferred Exchange:__ Ruble volatility; capital controls; credit markets constricted; cryptocurrency adoption rises as alternative value transfer.<br><br>
__Surplus Extraction & Redistribution:__ War expenditure absorbs fiscal surplus; social spending maintained but squeezed; extraction load on productive base intensifies.<br><br>
__Wealth Distribution & Asset Defense:__ Oligarch assets abroad frozen; domestic elite shift holdings to jurisdiction-proof instruments; inequality stable but asset security uncertain.<br><br>
__Byproducts & Waste Routing:__ Accelerated e-waste from obsolete equipment lacking Western parts; disposal infrastructure strained; routing to landfill predominates.<br><br>
## __P04. Social Environment__
__Social Trust & Mutual Aid:__ Wartime polarization deepens societal cleavages; trust radius contracts to immediate kin and vetted circles; mutual aid among war-affected families increases locally.<br><br>
__Stratification, Status & Group Identity:__ Pro-war and anti-war identities harden; ethnic minorities disproportionately mobilized; emigrant diaspora forms distinct identity stratum.<br><br>
__Kinship, Family & Inheritance:__ Mobilization disrupts household formation; inheritance patterns unchanged formally but wartime deaths shift intergenerational transfers.<br><br>
__Civic Association & Self-Organization:__ Independent civil society effectively eliminated; remaining associations either co-opted or operate in exile; associational fabric hollowed.<br><br>
__Leisure, Play & Sociability:__ Domestic entertainment platforms replace Western streaming; gaming shifts to pirated or domestically licensed titles; sociability retreats online under surveillance.<br><br>
__Grievance Channels & Social Discharge:__ Protest criminalized; grievance channels sealed; discontent accumulates unexpressed except in private digital spaces surveilled by SORM.<br><br>
## __P05. Political Environment__
__Power Structure & Authority:__ Executive power maximally concentrated; war emergency justifies decree rule; regional governors subordinated; centralization at wartime peak.<br><br>
__Succession & Power Transfer:__ No succession mechanism tested; constitutional term limits bypassed; transfer question deferred indefinitely under war conditions.<br><br>
__Legitimacy & Political Narrative:__ Great Patriotic War mythology mobilized; "denazification" narrative propagated domestically; legitimacy among war supporters stable, among dissenters collapsed.<br><br>
__Administrative Capacity & Institutional Integrity:__ State capacity adequate for repression and mobilization; corruption embedded but functional; digital governance systems (Gosuslugi) operate domestically.<br><br>
__Political Participation & Mobilization:__ Electoral competition eliminated; participation reduced to plebiscitary endorsement; mobilization channeled through state-managed "volunteer" structures.<br><br>
__Geopolitical Position & External Relations:__ Rupture with West complete; dependency on China deepens; relations with Global South cultivated; diplomatic isolation from G7 absolute.<br><br>
__Coercive Apparatus & Means of Force:__ Military stretched by Ukrainian front; internal security forces (Rosgvardia, FSB) maintain domestic control; digital surveillance apparatus expanded.<br><br>
__Internal Order & Threat Environment:__ Dissent suppressed; terrorism risk from Ukrainian operations; internal order maintained through fear; disorder margin low but coercion load high.<br><br>
## __P06. Informational Environment__
__Channels & Transmission Velocity:__ Global internet connectivity degraded by platform bans; VPN use widespread but contested; domestic channels (VK, Telegram, Yandex) dominant; velocity within splinternet high, across it throttled.<br><br>
__Record, Storage & Persistence:__ State archives intact; private cloud services (Google, Apple) inaccessible; data localization mandate forces domestic storage; persistence dependent on state-controlled infrastructure.<br><br>
__Framing, Censorship & Discourse Norms:__ "War" legally forbidden as descriptor; independent media banned; discourse norms enforced by criminal prosecution; permitted band narrowest since Soviet era.<br><br>
__Reception, Attention & Amplification:__ State television dominates older demographics; younger audiences fragment across Telegram channels; amplification controlled by algorithmic manipulation on VK and Yandex.<br><br>
__Signal Integrity & Noise Load:__ Disinformation volume extreme; verification capacity overwhelmed; signal-to-noise ratio collapsed for domestic audience; foreign observers rely on OSINT and exiled sources.<br><br>
## __P07. Judicial Environment__
__Moral Foundations & Sense of Justice:__ Patriotic duty overrides liberal rights discourse; justice framed as defense of motherland; dissenters cast as traitors deserving punishment.<br><br>
__Law, Codification & Regulatory Evolution:__ "Fake news" laws criminalize deviation from official narrative; foreign-agent designations expanded; regulatory framework weaponized against dissent.<br><br>
__Adjudication, Enforcement & Liability:__ Courts rubber-stamp prosecutions; conviction rates near 100% in political cases; enforcement swift and arbitrary; liability for online speech severe.<br><br>
__Rights, Protections & Compliance Burden:__ Press freedom, assembly, speech effectively nullified; compliance burden on platforms extreme; data localization and content filtering mandatory.<br><br>
__Impunity & Unredressed Wrong:__ State actors enjoy complete impunity; war crimes uninvestigated domestically; wrongful convictions accumulate; redress unavailable.<br><br>
## __P08. Intellectual Environment__
__Inquiry, Theory & Paradigm:__ Scientific collaboration with West severed; journal access restricted; brain drain accelerates; research frontier recedes in cutting-edge fields.<br><br>
__Applied Knowledge & Technical Method:__ Reverse engineering of Western technology intensifies; import substitution programs launched but lag; technical method degrades without access to updates.<br><br>
__Education, Knowledge Stock & Skill Distribution:__ Universities lose faculty to emigration; STEM education continues but quality declines; knowledge stock erodes as international exchange halts.<br><br>
__Obsolescence & Knowledge Decay:__ Software without updates becomes insecure; hardware without parts degrades; knowledge decay accelerates as practitioners emigrate; technical debt accumulates.<br><br>
## __P09. Cultural Environment__
__Mythos, Memory & Foundational Identity:__ World War II victory cult intensified; imperial nostalgia mobilized; identity narrative hardened around siege mentality and civilizational distinctiveness.<br><br>
__Artistic Expression & Material Culture:__ Western cultural products pirated or banned; domestic production increases under state patronage; material culture increasingly isolated from global trends.<br><br>
__Collective Mood & Behavioral Disposition:__ Public mood oscillates between patriotic mobilization and private anxiety; behavioral compliance enforced; risk aversion extreme regarding political expression.<br><br>
__Form Exhaustion & Meaning Decay:__ Soviet-era forms revived lose potency through repetition; "Z" symbol rapidly becomes hollow signifier; meaning decay accelerates as official discourse diverges from lived experience.<br><br>
## __P10. Spiritual Environment__
__Cosmology & Existential Outlook:__ Russian Orthodox Church endorses war as holy cause; fatalism prevalent; secular doubt suppressed; existential outlook framed as civilizational survival.<br><br>
__Ritual, Devotion & Sacred Practice:__ Church attendance stable; war memorials sacralized; devotional practice intertwined with patriotic observance; sacred and national fused.<br><br>
__Religious Institutions & Doctrinal Authority:__ Patriarch Kirill consolidated as state ally; Ukrainian Orthodox Church autocephaly contested; doctrinal authority weaponized against "Western decadence."<br><br>
__Spiritual Diffusion, Syncretism & Displacement:__ Orthodox nationalism dominant; minority faiths marginalized; spiritual alternatives suppressed; diffusion inward toward traditional Orthodoxy.<br><br>
__Disenchantment & Sacred Discharge:__ Private disenchantment masked by public conformity; existential void among emigres and dissenters unaddressed by either secular or religious frameworks; sacred discharge incomplete.<br><br>
