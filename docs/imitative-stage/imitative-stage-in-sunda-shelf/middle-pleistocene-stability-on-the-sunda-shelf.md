# __500,000 BCE – 300,000 BCE__ — Middle Pleistocene Stability on the Sunda Shelf

__Climate Regime & Volatility:__ Tropical climate with seasonal monsoon rainfall; glacial-interglacial oscillations producing modest cooling and drying during glacials; overall regime stable within bounds tolerable for tropical forest adaptation.<br><br>
__Hazard Regime & Exposure:__ Active volcanism along Java's arc producing periodic ashfall and pyroclastic hazards; earthquakes frequent; flood pulses in riverine lowlands; populations adapted to chronic hazard exposure with margin against individual events.<br><br>
__Resource & Energy Endowment:__ Abundant volcanic stone for tool manufacture; freshwater from perennial rivers; diverse tropical forest and riverine biomass; resource base ample relative to hominin population density.<br><br>
__Waste Absorption & Sink Capacity:__ Negligible waste production from small hominin groups; tropical forest and riverine systems absorbing all organic refuse with effectively unlimited absorptive capacity; no environmental degradation detectable.<br><br>
