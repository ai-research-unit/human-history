# __850,000 BCE – 750,000 BCE__ — The Gran Dolina TD6 Population
__Summary:__ A skeletal assemblage of at least six Homo antecessor individuals preserved in level TD6 of Gran Dolina, Atapuerca, with clear evidence of anthropophagous processing. The population sample spans infants to adults, offering a rare window into hominin biology and behaviour. Mode 1 lithics accompany the remains. The binding constraint is the fragility of small-group demography with high juvenile mortality. Margin against extinction is thin; population turnover is the dominant dynamic.
