# __130,000 BCE – 100,000 BCE__ — Interglacial Expansion and Maba Cave
__Summary:__ A static snapshot of archaic Homo sapiens presence in southern China during Marine Isotope Stage 5, the Last Interglacial — warm, wet monsoon conditions permit expansion into subtropical karst landscapes; the Maba cranium in Guangdong Province represents the southernmost archaic sapiens yet documented in the region; population density remains extremely low with wide territorial margins; the binding constraint is the same as prior windows: embodied knowledge held in few heads and transmitted only by direct demonstration.
## __P01. Physical Environment__
__Geography & Spatial Friction:__ Subtropical karst terrain of Guangdong Province provides abundant cave shelters along river valleys draining to the South China Sea; dense monsoon forest imposes high friction on overland movement but river corridors ease transit.<br><br>
__Climate Regime & Volatility:__ MIS 5 interglacial warmth brings intensified East Asian monsoon, elevated rainfall, and mild winters with minimal frost; interannual variability moderate; conditions near the warm pole of Pleistocene oscillation.<br><br>
__Hazard Regime & Exposure:__ Typhoon exposure along the coast, seasonal flooding in valley bottoms, and occasional wildfire in drier interior zones; cave habitation buffers against storm and flood, leaving wide safety margins.<br><br>
__Resource & Energy Endowment:__ Limestone caves offer shelter; local chert nodules and river cobbles supply raw material for simple flake tools; subtropical forest provides diverse plant and animal foods; resource base effectively unlimited relative to sparse population.<br><br>
__Waste Absorption & Sink Capacity:__ Negligible waste load from small foraging groups; cave deposits accumulate bone and lithic debris at imperceptible rates; absorptive capacity of the subtropical landscape effectively unbounded.<br><br>
## __P02. Biological Environment__
__Pathogen Load & Mortality Baseline:__ Warm, humid conditions favor endemic parasites and waterborne pathogens; small dispersed bands limit epidemic transmission; mortality remains dominated by trauma, predation, and nutritional stress rather than infectious outbreaks.<br><br>
__Nutrition & Physiological Vitality:__ Subtropical forest diversity provides year-round plant foods and medium-bodied game; nutritional stress episodic rather than chronic; the Maba individual shows robust cranial architecture consistent with adequate nutrition.<br><br>
__Reproductive Biology & Pairing Dynamics:__ Pair-bonding and cooperative child-rearing within small bands; reproductive biology indistinguishable from other late archaic populations; no selective pressure distinct from baseline hominin pattern documented.<br><br>
__Demographic Renewal & Density:__ Population density extremely low across subtropical southern China; scattered bands occupy favorable cave and riverine locales; replacement rates sufficient to sustain thin occupation without expansion pressure.<br><br>
__Attrition & Dependency Overhang:__ Short lifespans limit accumulation of aged dependents; orphaned juveniles absorbed within band; dependency load light relative to productive capacity; no evidence of attrition stress.<br><br>
## __P03. Economic Environment__
__Subsistence & Provisioning Floor:__ Mixed foraging economy exploiting forest mammals, freshwater resources, tubers, and fruits; provisioning floor comfortably above starvation threshold; household pooling within band structure provides short-term buffering.<br><br>
__Energy Capture & Conversion:__ Human muscle and simple fire remain sole energy converters; per-capita work ceiling unchanged from prior windows; fire used for warmth, cooking, and predator deterrence.<br><br>
__Labor Organization & Coercion:__ Task division by age and sex within cooperative bands; no coercion beyond kinship obligation; labor organization unchanged from ancestral foraging pattern.<br><br>
__Production & Surplus Capacity:__ Production limited to immediate consumption; no storable surplus beyond a day's carry; capacity matches need without accumulation.<br><br>
__Storage, Reserves & Buffering:__ No durable storage technology; buffering relies on body fat reserves, landscape knowledge, and social sharing; adequate for seasonal fluctuation in subtropical forest.<br><br>
__Exchange, Trade & Logistics:__ Exchange limited to within-band sharing and occasional inter-band transfer of mates or raw materials; no evidence of long-distance logistics; raw materials locally sourced.<br><br>
__Value Storage, Credit & Deferred Exchange:__ Deferred reciprocity within kin networks substitutes for stored value; no external medium of exchange; obligation memory held in individual minds.<br><br>
__Surplus Extraction & Redistribution:__ No coordinating authority extracts surplus; communal sharing of kills and gathered foods distributes resources informally within the band.<br><br>
__Wealth Distribution & Asset Defense:__ Effective material equality; portable tool kits personally held; no durable property concentration; no defensive strategies necessary.<br><br>
__Byproducts & Waste Routing:__ Knapping debris and food remains accumulate at cave sites; volumes trivial; waste dispersed on landscape or left in situ without routing decisions.<br><br>

__Stratification, Status & Group Identity:__ Minimal stratification; status earned through demonstrated skill and generosity; group identity tied to shared territory and kinship; no rigid boundaries.<br><br>
__Kinship, Family & Inheritance:__ Kinship reckoned through both maternal and paternal lines; pair-bonds embedded in band structure; inheritance limited to transmissible skills and personal tools passed hand to hand.<br><br>
__Civic Association & Self-Organization:__ The band itself is the sole organizational unit; no secondary associations; norms enforced through direct social pressure and observation.<br><br>
__Leisure, Play & Sociability:__ Non-work time filled with social grooming, play among juveniles, and communal rest at cave sites; shared sociability reinforces bonds.<br><br>
__Grievance Channels & Social Discharge:__ Disputes resolved through avoidance, mediation by senior kin, or fission of the band; grievance load light given small group size; no standing instability documented.<br><br>
