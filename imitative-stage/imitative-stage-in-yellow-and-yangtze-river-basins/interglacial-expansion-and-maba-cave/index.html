    130,000 BCE – 100,000 BCE — Interglacial Expansion and Maba Cave

    Summary: A static snapshot of archaic Homo sapiens presence in southern China during Marine Isotope Stage 5, the Last Interglacial — warm, wet monsoon conditions permit expansion into subtropical karst landscapes; the Maba cranium in Guangdong Province represents the southernmost archaic sapiens yet documented in the region; population density remains extremely low with wide territorial margins; the binding constraint is the same as prior windows: embodied knowledge held in few heads and transmitted only by direct demonstration.

    P01. Physical Environment

    Geography & Spatial Friction: Subtropical karst terrain of Guangdong Province provides abundant cave shelters along river valleys draining to the South China Sea; dense monsoon forest imposes high friction on overland movement but river corridors ease transit.

    Climate Regime & Volatility: MIS 5 interglacial warmth brings intensified East Asian monsoon, elevated rainfall, and mild winters with minimal frost; interannual variability moderate; conditions near the warm pole of Pleistocene oscillation.

    Hazard Regime & Exposure: Typhoon exposure along the coast, seasonal flooding in valley bottoms, and occasional wildfire in drier interior zones; cave habitation buffers against storm and flood, leaving wide safety margins.

    Resource & Energy Endowment: Limestone caves offer shelter; local chert nodules and river cobbles supply raw material for simple flake tools; subtropical forest provides diverse plant and animal foods; resource base effectively unlimited relative to sparse population.

    Waste Absorption & Sink Capacity: Negligible waste load from small foraging groups; cave deposits accumulate bone and lithic debris at imperceptible rates; absorptive capacity of the subtropical landscape effectively unbounded.

    P02. Biological Environment

    Pathogen Load & Mortality Baseline: Warm, humid conditions favor endemic parasites and waterborne pathogens; small dispersed bands limit epidemic transmission; mortality remains dominated by trauma, predation, and nutritional stress rather than infectious outbreaks.

    Nutrition & Physiological Vitality: Subtropical forest diversity provides year-round plant foods and medium-bodied game; nutritional stress episodic rather than chronic; the Maba individual shows robust cranial architecture consistent with adequate nutrition.

    Reproductive Biology & Pairing Dynamics: Pair-bonding and cooperative child-rearing within small bands; reproductive biology indistinguishable from other late archaic populations; no selective pressure distinct from baseline hominin pattern documented.

    Demographic Renewal & Density: Population density extremely low across subtropical southern China; scattered bands occupy favorable cave and riverine locales; replacement rates sufficient to sustain thin occupation without expansion pressure.

    Attrition & Dependency Overhang: Short lifespans limit accumulation of aged dependents; orphaned juveniles absorbed within band; dependency load light relative to productive capacity; no evidence of attrition stress.

    P03. Economic Environment

    Subsistence & Provisioning Floor: Mixed foraging economy exploiting forest mammals, freshwater resources, tubers, and fruits; provisioning floor comfortably above starvation threshold; household pooling within band structure provides short-term buffering.

    Energy Capture & Conversion: Human muscle and simple fire remain sole energy converters; per-capita work ceiling unchanged from prior windows; fire used for warmth, cooking, and predator deterrence.

    Labor Organization & Coercion: Task division by age and sex within cooperative bands; no coercion beyond kinship obligation; labor organization unchanged from ancestral foraging pattern.

    Production & Surplus Capacity: Production limited to immediate consumption; no storable surplus beyond a day's carry; capacity matches need without accumulation.

    Storage, Reserves & Buffering: No durable storage technology; buffering relies on body fat reserves, landscape knowledge, and social sharing; adequate for seasonal fluctuation in subtropical forest.

    Exchange, Trade & Logistics: Exchange limited to within-band sharing and occasional inter-band transfer of mates or raw materials; no evidence of long-distance logistics; raw materials locally sourced.

    Value Storage, Credit & Deferred Exchange: Deferred reciprocity within kin networks substitutes for stored value; no external medium of exchange; obligation memory held in individual minds.

    Surplus Extraction & Redistribution: No coordinating authority extracts surplus; communal sharing of kills and gathered foods distributes resources informally within the band.

    Wealth Distribution & Asset Defense: Effective material equality; portable tool kits personally held; no durable property concentration; no defensive strategies necessary.

    Byproducts & Waste Routing: Knapping debris and food remains accumulate at cave sites; volumes trivial; waste dispersed on landscape or left in situ without routing decisions.

    P04. Social Environment

    Social Trust & Mutual Aid: High within-band trust sustained by face-to-face interaction and kinship; reciprocity radius extends to the band and immediate neighboring groups; trust baseline stable.

    Stratification, Status & Group Identity: Minimal stratification; status earned through demonstrated skill and generosity; group identity tied to shared territory and kinship; no rigid boundaries.

    Kinship, Family & Inheritance: Kinship reckoned through both maternal and paternal lines; pair-bonds embedded in band structure; inheritance limited to transmissible skills and personal tools passed hand to hand.

    Civic Association & Self-Organization: The band itself is the sole organizational unit; no secondary associations; norms enforced through direct social pressure and observation.

    Leisure, Play & Sociability: Non-work time filled with social grooming, play among juveniles, and communal rest at cave sites; shared sociability reinforces bonds.

    Grievance Channels & Social Discharge: Disputes resolved through avoidance, mediation by senior kin, or fission of the band; grievance load light given small group size; no standing instability documented.

    P05. Political Environment

    Power Structure & Authority: Leadership situational and informal; authority accrues to skilled individuals for specific tasks; no permanent offices; power diffuse and consensual.

    Succession & Power Transfer: No formal succession; situational leadership shifts with context; transfer a non-issue in the absence of offices.

    Legitimacy & Political Narrative: Legitimacy rests on demonstrated competence and generosity; no narrative justification required beyond immediate reputation.

    Administrative Capacity & Institutional Integrity: No administrative apparatus; coordination achieved through direct communication and imitation; no records or institutional memory beyond living minds.

    Political Participation & Mobilization: All adult band members participate in collective decisions through face-to-face discussion and consensus; no formal mobilization structures.

    Geopolitical Position & External Relations: Inter-band relations governed by kinship ties and occasional encounters; no territorial warfare documented; external pressure minimal.

    Coercive Apparatus & Means of Force: No specialized coercive apparatus; violence personal and sporadic; weaponry limited to hunting tools repurposed for interpersonal conflict.

    Internal Order & Threat Environment: Internal disorder rare; predation by large carnivores and inter-group violence are occasional threats; band mobility serves as primary response to threat.

    P06. Informational Environment

    Channels & Transmission Velocity: Information moves only at the speed of human movement and within earshot of direct demonstration; transmission strictly imitative — gesture, posture, guided practice — with no linguistic encoding.

    Record, Storage & Persistence: No external record; all knowledge persists in living memory and embodied habit; loss of skilled individuals erases knowledge irreversibly.

    Framing, Censorship & Discourse Norms: No discourse in the linguistic sense; imitative display conveys technique without propositional framing; no censorship applicable.

    Reception, Attention & Amplification: Attention constrained to immediate social circle; amplification limited to repeated demonstration; no broadcast capacity beyond the co-present group.

    Signal Integrity & Noise Load: Fidelity depends on the demonstrator's skill and the observer's attention; copying error accumulates across generations but is self-correcting through functional feedback; noise load negligible.

    P07. Judicial Environment

    Moral Foundations & Sense of Justice: Proto-moral intuitions around fairness, reciprocity, and harm avoidance shape social expectations; no codified ethics but behavioral norms enforced through social sanction.

    Law, Codification & Regulatory Evolution: No formal law; unwritten taboos and customary expectations govern conduct; regulatory evolution imperceptible.

    Adjudication, Enforcement & Liability: Disputes adjudicated informally by senior kin or resolved through avoidance; no third-party enforcement apparatus; liability assigned through reputational consequence.

    Rights, Protections & Compliance Burden: No articulated rights; protection derives from kin solidarity and group membership; compliance burden negligible given simple norms.

    Impunity & Unredressed Wrong: Serious wrongs may go unredressed if perpetrator departs or group fissions; impunity limited by small scale and high interdependence; residue minimal.

    P08. Intellectual Environment

    Inquiry, Theory & Paradigm: No formal inquiry or explanatory theory; understanding of environment tacit and procedural, embedded in practice rather than articulated.

    Applied Knowledge & Technical Method: Lithic reduction techniques, fire management, and foraging strategies constitute applied knowledge; methods transmitted by demonstration; no abstracted blueprints.

    Education, Knowledge Stock & Skill Distribution: Skill transmitted from experienced to novice through observation and guided practice; knowledge stock limited to band repertoire; distribution even among adults.

    Obsolescence & Knowledge Decay: Knowledge decays with death of skilled practitioners; no archive preserves technique; obsolescence and loss proceed at biological pace, partly offset by functional relearning.

    P09. Cultural Environment

    Mythos, Memory & Foundational Identity: No linguistic mythos; collective memory limited to shared experience of living individuals; identity rooted in territory and kin rather than narrative.

    Artistic Expression & Material Culture: Material culture consists of expedient flake tools and fire-hardened wood; no decorative elaboration or symbolic marking documented at Maba or contemporaneous sites.

    Collective Mood & Behavioral Disposition: Behavioral disposition oriented toward cautious exploration of subtropical environment; confidence supported by interglacial abundance; no evidence of widespread anxiety.

    Form Exhaustion & Meaning Decay: No symbolic forms to exhaust; meaning inheres in functional practice; decay occurs only as skill is lost, not as symbol empties.

    P10. Spiritual Environment

    Cosmology & Existential Outlook: No recoverable cosmology; existential orientation presumably immediate and practical; any proto-spiritual awareness leaves no archaeological trace.

    Ritual, Devotion & Sacred Practice: No documented ritual behavior; mortuary treatment unknown at Maba; sacred practice, if present, indistinguishable from practical routine.

    Religious Institutions & Doctrinal Authority: No institutions or doctrinal authority; concept inapplicable to pre-linguistic bands.

    Spiritual Diffusion, Syncretism & Displacement: No spiritual system to diffuse or displace; field null for this window.

    Disenchantment & Sacred Discharge: No prior enchantment to lose; existential residue, if any, undetectable; field null for this window.
