    70,000 BCE – 50,000 BCE — Late Mousterian Contraction and Population Turnover

    Summary: A twenty-thousand-year trend of demographic thinning and range contraction among Levantine Neanderthals under mounting MIS 4–early MIS 3 climatic stress; the window opens with sparse but persistent Mousterian occupation and closes on the threshold of population replacement, the binding constraint being the narrowing resource base that pushes local carrying capacity below replacement and leaves no margin against further cooling or competition.

    P01. Physical Environment

    Geography & Spatial Friction: The Jordan Rift corridor and Mount Carmel karst remain fixed; coastal plains narrow during low sea stands; interior steppes expand while highland refugia contract, increasing transit friction between shrinking habitable patches.

    Climate Regime & Volatility: MIS 4 cooling intensifies early in the window, bringing colder winters and reduced rainfall; interannual variability rises; by 55 ka early MIS 3 warming begins but remains unstable with frequent reversals.

    Hazard Regime & Exposure: No evidence of catastrophic volcanic or seismic disruption within the window; exposure to drought and cold snaps is chronic rather than acute, but buffering capacity is thin given small group sizes.

    Resource & Energy Endowment: Ungulate biomass declines as steppe expands and woodland contracts; nut-bearing trees retreat to refugia; raw-material outcrops remain accessible but are exploited by fewer and more dispersed groups.

    Waste Absorption & Sink Capacity: Anthropogenic discharge negligible; open-landscape dispersal and cave abandonment leave no measurable sink saturation; capacity effectively unlimited relative to load.

    P02. Biological Environment

    Pathogen Load & Mortality Baseline: No direct evidence of epidemic disease; baseline mortality likely elevated by nutritional stress and cold exposure; skeletal trauma frequencies at Amud and Kebara suggest high accident rates.

    Nutrition & Physiological Vitality: Dental wear and enamel hypoplasia in late Neanderthal remains indicate episodic nutritional stress; body mass and robusticity persist but energetic demands rise under colder conditions.

    Reproductive Biology & Pairing Dynamics: Small, dispersed groups imply reduced mate availability and elevated inbreeding risk; no direct demographic data, but genetic evidence from European Neanderthals suggests low effective population sizes.

    Demographic Renewal & Density: Population density declining throughout the window; site occupation frequency and intensity drop after 60 ka; by 50 ka Neanderthal presence approaches or reaches zero in the Levant.

    Attrition & Dependency Overhang: Shortened adult lifespans inferred from skeletal aging; high juvenile mortality probable; dependency load remains proportional to productive cohort, but both are shrinking.

    P03. Economic Environment

    Subsistence & Provisioning Floor: Hunting focused on medium ungulates — fallow deer, gazelle, aurochs — with intensified marrow extraction and carcass processing; provisioning floor tightens as prey density falls.

    Energy Capture & Conversion: Fire use habitual; hearths documented at Kebara and Amud; conversion efficiency stable but total energy throughput declining with reduced prey availability and smaller group sizes.

    Labor Organization & Coercion: Task differentiation minimal and inferred only from spatial patterning in cave deposits; labor mobilized through imitative coordination within kin groups; no coercion beyond immediate social pressure.

    Production & Surplus Capacity: Production limited to immediate consumption and tool replacement; surplus capacity negligible; no evidence of storage beyond short-term caching.

    Storage, Reserves & Buffering: No durable storage technology; buffering relies on body fat and opportunistic caching; reserves inadequate against multi-season shortfalls.

    Exchange, Trade & Logistics: Raw-material sourcing remains local to regional, with flint transport rarely exceeding thirty kilometers; no evidence of inter-group exchange networks.

    Value Storage, Credit & Deferred Exchange: Absent; all reciprocity immediate and embedded in kin-based food sharing.

    Surplus Extraction & Redistribution: No surplus to extract; communal sharing of large kills is inferred but undocumented in this window.

    Wealth Distribution & Asset Defense: No durable assets beyond portable toolkits; distribution effectively egalitarian by default; no defense required.

    Byproducts & Waste Routing: Knapping debris and bone refuse discarded in occupation zones; dispersed into landscape upon abandonment; no routing constraint.

    P04. Social Environment

    Social Trust & Mutual Aid: Trust radius limited to band membership; mutual aid presumed within kin groups; no evidence of inter-band cooperation or alliance.

    Stratification, Status & Group Identity: Status differentiation minimal and likely age- or skill-based; group identity tied to shared residence and kin; no material markers of rank.

    Kinship, Family & Inheritance: Kinship structures inferred from burial associations at Amud (adult with infant); inheritance of material goods trivial; knowledge transmitted imitatively within family units.

    Civic Association & Self-Organization: Band-level coordination only; no supra-band institutions; decision-making embedded in face-to-face interaction.

    Leisure, Play & Sociability: Sparse attestation; no preserved artifacts of play; sociability confined to camp and foraging-group contexts.

    Grievance Channels & Social Discharge: Interpersonal disputes resolved within band; fission of groups a likely outlet; unmetabolized grievance minimal given small group sizes.

    P05. Political Environment

    Power Structure & Authority: No formal authority; leadership situational and task-specific; power diffuse and consensual within small bands.

    Succession & Power Transfer: No institutionalized succession; influence accrues to experienced individuals and dissipates at death; no recoverable transfer mechanism.

    Legitimacy & Political Narrative: Absent in any recoverable form; no symbolic justification of authority.

    Administrative Capacity & Institutional Integrity: No administration; coordination achieved through imitative participation and habitual routine.

    Political Participation & Mobilization: All adults participate in subsistence decisions; mobilization for collective action is ad hoc and direct.

    Geopolitical Position & External Relations: No polities; inter-group relations sparsely attested; possible avoidance or low-level competition with other bands.

    Coercive Apparatus & Means of Force: No specialized weapons for inter-human violence; hunting equipment doubles as defensive capacity; force capacity distributed equally.

    Internal Order & Threat Environment: Disorder minimal; threat environment dominated by predators and environmental hazards rather than human conflict.

    P06. Informational Environment

    Channels & Transmission Velocity: All information moves through face-to-face demonstration; reach limited to visual and gestural range; velocity constrained by physical co-presence.

    Record, Storage & Persistence: Living memory alone; no durable encoding; knowledge persists only as long as knowledgeable individuals survive and transmit imitatively.

    Framing, Censorship & Discourse Norms: Absent in recoverable form; no symbolic discourse to frame or censor.

    Reception, Attention & Amplification: Attention directed by immediate demonstration; no amplification beyond direct observation; reception limited to co-present learners.

    Signal Integrity & Noise Load: Signal high-fidelity within demonstration context; noise introduced by incomplete observation or failed imitation; no verification mechanism beyond repeated practice.

    P07. Judicial Environment

    Moral Foundations & Sense of Justice: Inferred baseline sense of fairness in food sharing; no recoverable moral code beyond immediate reciprocity norms.

    Law, Codification & Regulatory Evolution: Absent; no formal rules; conduct governed by imitative habit and social pressure.

    Adjudication, Enforcement & Liability: No third-party adjudication; disputes settled interpersonally or by group pressure; liability concept not recoverable.

    Rights, Protections & Compliance Burden: No codified rights; autonomy constrained only by group norms; compliance burden negligible.

    Impunity & Unredressed Wrong: Wrongs addressed through direct retaliation or avoidance; no formal redress; residue likely minimal given band intimacy.

    P08. Intellectual Environment

    Inquiry, Theory & Paradigm: No formal inquiry; understanding of environment accumulated through trial, observation, and imitative transmission; no explanatory paradigm recoverable.

    Applied Knowledge & Technical Method: Levallois and prepared-core techniques persist without significant innovation; hafting inferred; technical method stable and imitatively reproduced.

    Education, Knowledge Stock & Skill Distribution: Knowledge transmitted hand-to-hand; skill distribution universal among adults; stock declining as population contracts and transmission chains break.

    Obsolescence & Knowledge Decay: Technique loss possible as groups disappear; no evidence of innovation compensating for attrition; net knowledge stock likely contracting toward window close.

    P09. Cultural Environment

    Mythos, Memory & Foundational Identity: No symbolic encoding; identity rooted in shared practice and kin association; foundational narrative absent or irrecoverable.

    Artistic Expression & Material Culture: Material culture limited to functional lithics and occasional ochre use (possible, sparsely attested); no representational art; aesthetic dimension minimal.

    Collective Mood & Behavioral Disposition: Inferred pragmatic focus on survival; no evidence of collective anxiety or confidence beyond that embedded in subsistence behavior.

    Form Exhaustion & Meaning Decay: No symbolic forms to exhaust; Mousterian toolkit stable but not renewed; stasis rather than decay.

    P10. Spiritual Environment

    Cosmology & Existential Outlook: No recoverable cosmology; existence oriented to immediate survival and kin bonds; metaphysical outlook inaccessible.

    Ritual, Devotion & Sacred Practice: Burial of dead attested at Amud and Kebara; whether this constitutes ritual or practical disposal debated; no grave goods indicating devotional intent.

    Religious Institutions & Doctrinal Authority: Absent; no institutional religion; no doctrinal authority.

    Spiritual Diffusion, Syncretism & Displacement: No recoverable spiritual system to diffuse or displace; question moot for this window.

    Disenchantment & Sacred Discharge: Concept inapplicable; no prior enchantment to erode; spiritual residue not measurable.
