    500000 BCE – 300000 BCE — Humid-Arid Oscillation Regime

    Summary: A 200,000-year snapshot of the dynamic equilibrium governing Northwest African environments: precession-driven (~23,000-year) monsoon cycles produce recurring North African Humid Periods interspersed with arid phases; during humid windows the Sahara greens and hominin dispersal corridors open; during arid phases desert barriers reform and populations contract to refugia; the binding constraint is the insolation threshold that triggers rapid state-switches between green and desert conditions; the margin lies in the coastal and montane refugia that buffer populations through arid phases; direction of change is oscillatory rather than linear.

    P01. Physical Environment

    Geography & Spatial Friction: The Sahara functions as a dynamic barrier — expanding during arid phases to block north-south transit, contracting during humid phases to expose lake basins, reactivated wadis, and grassland corridors; Atlas Mountains and Atlantic coast remain accessible throughout.

    Climate Regime & Volatility: Precession (~23,000-year) drives primary oscillation; obliquity (~41,000-year) and eccentricity (~100,000-year) modulate amplitude; glacial maxima suppress humid periods; volatility is the regime itself — threshold behavior produces rapid transitions once insolation crosses critical values.

    Hazard Regime & Exposure: Aridification is the primary hazard; multi-millennial dry phases eliminate interior water sources and collapse grassland productivity; coastal and Atlas refugia provide buffer but interior exposure is extreme during desert maxima.

    Resource & Energy Endowment: During humid phases: extensive lakes (paleo-Chad, paleo-Saharan basins), permanent rivers, grassland game, fish, and plant resources; during arid phases: endowment contracts to perennial springs, coastal shellfish, and montane water sources.

    Waste Absorption & Sink Capacity: Negligible anthropogenic load; dust flux increases dramatically during arid phases (recorded in ocean sediment cores) as Saharan deflation intensifies; sink capacity unlimited for minimal hominin discharge.

    P02. Biological Environment

    Pathogen Load & Mortality Baseline: Humid phases may increase waterborne pathogen exposure near lakes and marshes; arid phases reduce disease but increase physiological stress from heat and water scarcity; net mortality pressure oscillates with climate.

    Nutrition & Physiological Vitality: Nutritional status improves during Green Sahara phases when game, fish, and plant foods proliferate; declines during arid phases as populations concentrate at refugia and resource competition intensifies.

    Reproductive Biology & Pairing Dynamics: No direct evidence; reproductive success likely tracks resource availability, expanding during humid phases and contracting during arid stress; pair-bonding and cooperative breeding patterns inferred stable.

    Demographic Renewal & Density: Population expands and disperses during humid phases, occupying interior basins; contracts and concentrates during arid phases; regional population oscillates but lineages persist through refugia; metapopulation dynamics govern renewal.

    Attrition & Dependency Overhang: Arid phases likely increase juvenile mortality and reduce support for dependents as resources contract; humid phases relax these pressures; dependency load tracks carrying capacity oscillation.

    P03. Economic Environment

    Subsistence & Provisioning Floor: Foraging radius expands during humid phases to include savanna hunting, fishing, and diverse plant collection; contracts during arid phases to coastal and spring-fed oasis foraging; provisioning floor oscillates but remains above extinction threshold.

    Energy Capture & Conversion Regime: Human muscle and controlled fire; fire use facilitates landscape burning during humid phases (contested evidence); energy ceiling oscillates with resource availability but conversion technology unchanged.

    Labor Organization & Coercion: No formal organization or coercion; labor allocated through kinship and immediate cooperation; oscillation does not alter organizational form, only activity mix and mobility range.

    Production & Surplus Capacity: No durable surplus; production tracks immediate consumption; humid phases allow caloric abundance but no storage infrastructure to capitalize it; arid phases impose scarcity but no production technology change.

    Storage, Reserves & Risk-Buffering: No storage technology; risk buffered through mobility, landscape knowledge, and fat reserves in bodies rather than external caches; oscillation managed through spatial flexibility rather than stockpiling.

    Exchange, Trade & Logistics: Humid phases enable longer-distance raw material transport and inter-band contact as corridors open; arid phases restrict movement and isolate populations; exchange radius oscillates with climate.

    Value Storage, Credit & Deferred Exchange: No monetary or proto-monetary instruments; deferred reciprocity obligations may intensify during resource stress but leave no recoverable trace; no durable value storage.

    Surplus Extraction & Redistribution: No coordinating authority; communal sharing of kills and gathered foods; redistribution immediate and face-to-face; oscillation does not alter mechanism, only volume flowing through it.

    Wealth Distribution & Asset Defense: No accumulated wealth; oscillation does not produce or redistribute property; asset defense limited to bodily possession and kin alliance.

    Byproduct Streams & Material Discharge: Lithic debitage and food refuse at occupation sites; discharge minimal; humid-phase sites may show richer faunal remains; arid-phase sites sparser; discharge oscillates with occupation intensity.

    P04. Social Environment

    Social Trust & Mutual Aid: Trust networks critical for survival during arid-phase concentration at refugia; humid phases allow band dispersal and reduced interdependence; trust radius may expand during green phases as inter-band contact increases.

    Stratification, Status & Group Identity: No formal stratification; status and identity structures remain stable across oscillation; group boundaries may become more porous during humid phases when dispersal and mixing occur.

    Kinship, Family & Inheritance: Kinship structures unrecoverable; oscillation unlikely to alter fundamental kinship organization; humid phases may facilitate mate exchange between previously isolated bands.

    Civic Association & Self-Organization: Band-level organization throughout; oscillation may alter band size and composition as fusion occurs at refugia during arid phases and fission during humid dispersal.

    Leisure, Play & Sociability: Humid phases likely afford more leisure as resource abundance reduces foraging pressure; arid phases increase work-to-resource ratio; sociability patterns stable but time allocation shifts.

    Grievance Metabolization & Social Discharge: Arid-phase concentration may intensify interpersonal friction while reducing fission options; humid phases restore spatial solutions to grievance; discharge capacity oscillates with territory availability.

    P05. Political Environment

    Power Structure & Authority: No sovereign power; situational leadership unchanged by oscillation; authority remains diffuse, task-specific, and kinship-based throughout.

    Succession & Power Transfer: No formalized succession; oscillation does not alter leadership emergence or transfer patterns.

    Legitimacy & Political Narrative: No recoverable legitimacy discourse; oscillation produces no detectable ideological response at this pre-linguistic stage.

    Administrative Capacity & Institutional Integrity: No administrative apparatus; oscillation managed through embodied landscape knowledge and mobility decisions rather than institutional response.

    Political Participation & Mobilization: Direct participation continues; oscillation does not create exclusion or mobilization dynamics; band decisions remain face-to-face.

    Geopolitical Position & External Relations: Humid phases enable inter-regional contact and gene flow between Northwest African, Saharan, and sub-Saharan populations; arid phases isolate regions; no diplomatic structure but connectivity oscillates.

    Coercive Apparatus & Means of Force: No organized coercive capacity; oscillation does not alter force distribution or weaponry; interpersonal violence possible but not institutionalized.

    Internal Order & Threat Environment: Predation by large carnivores remains primary threat; arid-phase concentration may intensify inter-group competition at refugia (contested); no recoverable evidence of conflict intensification.

    P06. Informational Environment

    Channels & Transmission Velocity: Face-to-face demonstration and imitation; humid phases extend contact range as populations disperse and encounter others; arid phases contract networks and may accelerate local drift; velocity remains at walking pace.

    Record, Storage & Persistence: Living memory only; no external storage media; knowledge persists solely in practitioners' heads and hands; oscillation does not alter storage technology — lineage survival through refugia is the persistence mechanism.

    Framing, Censorship & Discourse Norms: Pre-linguistic context precludes propositional framing or censorship; behavioral norms transmitted through modeling; oscillation does not alter transmission mode.

    Reception, Attention & Amplification: Attention constrained to immediate demonstration; humid-phase aggregations may produce larger audiences for skill display; no amplification beyond physical co-presence.

    Signal Integrity & Noise Load: High fidelity in face-to-face transmission; genetic and cultural drift may accumulate during arid-phase isolation; humid phases re-homogenize through contact; signal integrity oscillates with connectivity.

    P07. Judicial Environment

    Moral Foundations & Sense of Justice: Inferred baseline reciprocity norms stable across oscillation; no recoverable moral discourse; sense of fairness likely present but pre-propositional and unchanged by climate shifts.

    Law, Codification & Regulatory Evolution: No formal law or codified rules; conduct governed by habitual practice; oscillation does not produce legal innovation — informative null.

    Adjudication, Enforcement & Liability: No formal adjudication machinery; disputes resolved through interpersonal means; no third-party arbiters or liability assignment mechanisms — informative null for this pre-institutional window.

    Rights, Protections & Compliance Burden: No formalized rights; individual autonomy constrained by kin obligation and resource pressure; compliance burden may intensify during arid-phase concentration but no formal system to generate it.

    Impunity & Unredressed Wrong: No formal justice system; wrongs addressed through direct response or separation; arid phases may reduce separation options and increase unmetabolized grievance; residue accumulates interpersonally.

    P08. Intellectual Environment

    Inquiry, Theory & Paradigm: No recoverable propositional inquiry; practical problem-solving embedded in foraging and tool-making; oscillation does not alter inquiry mode — environmental knowledge is procedural and embodied.

    Applied Knowledge & Technical Method: Acheulean technology continues through oscillation; no detectable innovation correlated with climate phases; technique stable, transmitted through imitation regardless of environmental state.

    Education, Knowledge Stock & Skill Distribution: Apprenticeship through observation continues; humid phases may expand the pool of teachers and learners through aggregation; arid phases concentrate knowledge in refugia populations — stock oscillates but method unchanged.

    Obsolescence & Knowledge Decay: Arid-phase population crashes may cause knowledge loss through lineage extinction; humid-phase dispersal may spread surviving techniques; decay risk highest during prolonged arid phases that reduce population below transmission threshold.

    P09. Cultural Environment

    Mythos, Memory & Foundational Identity: No recoverable mythos; group identity likely grounded in co-residence and shared practice rather than narrative; oscillation does not produce detectable identity shift at this pre-linguistic stage.

    Artistic Expression & Material Culture: Symmetrical Acheulean handaxes continue without detectable stylistic response to oscillation; no unambiguous art or ornamentation; material culture stable across climate phases.

    Collective Mood & Behavioral Disposition: Unrecoverable; inferred stress during arid phases and relaxation during humid phases; no behavioral residue distinguishable in archaeological record.

    Form Exhaustion & Meaning Decay: Not applicable in pre-symbolic context; technique evolves slowly without evidence of exhaustion or anomie; oscillation does not produce detectable cultural fatigue.
