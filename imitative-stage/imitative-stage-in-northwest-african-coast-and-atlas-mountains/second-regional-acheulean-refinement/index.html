    600000 BCE – 400000 BCE — Second Regional Acheulean Refinement

    Summary: A 200,000-year trend across the Atlantic Moroccan littoral during which Acheulean technology advances from irregular handaxes toward symmetrical, standardized forms; Homo cf. rhodesiensis populations occupy cave sites at Thomas Quarry I and Sidi Abderrahman; the binding constraint is cognitive and motor-skill transmission through imitation alone, gating the pace of refinement; climate oscillation supplies intermittent humid corridors but does not yet disrupt regional continuity; the margin lies in the predictability of coastal resources and the accumulated skill embedded in knapping tradition.

    P01. Physical Environment

    Geography & Spatial Friction: Atlantic Moroccan coast and Casablanca platform provide consolidated quartzite and flint outcrops; coastal caves and karstic cavities offer shelter; east-west transit blocked by Atlas foothills; north-south mobility constrained by desert expansion during arid phases.

    Climate Regime & Volatility: Precession-driven humid-arid oscillation (~23,000-year period) governs rainfall; interglacial phases bring open woodland-savanna; glacial maxima suppress monsoon penetration; volatility high but averaged over millennia the coastal belt remains habitable.

    Hazard Regime & Exposure: Low seismic and volcanic exposure; primary hazard is multi-millennial aridification pulses reducing interior water sources; coastal margin buffers these shocks through persistent groundwater and littoral resources.

    Resource & Energy Endowment: High-quality quartzite and flint for tool manufacture available in shore platforms and quarry exposures; perennial springs; coastal shellfish beds and savanna megafauna during humid phases.

    Waste Absorption & Sink Capacity: Negligible; small hominin populations produce minimal waste; organic refuse recycled by scavengers; lithic debitage accumulates but imposes no environmental load.

    P02. Biological Environment

    Pathogen Load & Mortality Baseline: Sparsely attested; inferred low endemic disease load in small, mobile bands; trauma and predation likely primary mortality factors; no epidemic signature in skeletal record.

    Nutrition & Physiological Vitality: Dental wear on Thomas Quarry specimens indicates coarse diet; body size inferred robust (~900-1100 cc cranial capacity); nutritional status adequate during humid phases, stressed during arid intervals.

    Reproductive Biology & Pairing Dynamics: No direct evidence; inferred flexible pair-bonding and cooperative breeding consistent with Homo erectus s.l. patterns; selective pressure toward extended juvenile dependency supporting skill transmission.

    Demographic Renewal & Density: Population density extremely low; regional population likely numbered hundreds to low thousands; replacement rate sufficient to maintain continuous occupation at Thomas Quarry and Sidi Abderrahman over multiple millennia.

    Attrition & Dependency Overhang: Inferred high juvenile mortality; limited evidence for support of aged or infirm; dependency load small relative to productive adults given short lifespans.

    P03. Economic Environment

    Subsistence & Provisioning Floor: Mobile foraging combining megafauna scavenging and hunting, plant collection, and probable coastal shellfish exploitation; household pooling through immediate-return sharing; margin adequate during humid phases.

    Energy Capture & Conversion Regime: Human muscle and controlled fire; fire use inferred but direct evidence sparse for this window; per-capita energy ceiling constrained to what bodies can extract and carry daily.

    Labor Organization & Coercion: No coercion beyond kin obligation; task division likely by age and sex; cooperative hunting and raw material transport requiring coordinated effort without formal specialization.

    Production & Surplus Capacity: No durable surplus; production limited to immediate consumption plus stone tool maintenance; surplus concept inapplicable to immediate-return foraging economy.

    Storage, Reserves & Risk-Buffering: No storage technology; risk buffered through mobility, landscape knowledge, and social sharing networks; no granaries or caches.

    Exchange, Trade & Logistics: Raw material transport up to tens of kilometers attested by non-local flint in assemblages; no market or formalized exchange; distribution through direct provisioning and reciprocity.

    Value Storage, Credit & Deferred Exchange: No monetary or proto-monetary instruments; deferred reciprocity obligations likely present but unrecoverable; no durable value storage.

    Surplus Extraction & Redistribution: No coordinating authority; communal allocation of large kills through immediate sharing; no tribute, tax, or centralized redistribution.

    Wealth Distribution & Asset Defense: No accumulated wealth beyond personal toolkit; no property concentration; asset defense limited to bodily possession of tools and immediate kin alliance.

    Byproduct Streams & Material Discharge: Lithic debitage and food refuse at occupation sites; discharge minimal and locally absorbed; no routing decision beyond abandonment in place.

    P04. Social Environment

    Social Trust & Mutual Aid: High within-band trust essential for cooperative foraging and tool-making transmission; trust radius probably limited to face-to-face acquaintances; mutual aid networks stable during this window.

    Stratification, Status & Group Identity: No formal stratification; status likely based on skill, age, and kinship; group identity defined by co-residence and shared repertoire; no evidence of inter-group boundary markers.

    Kinship, Family & Inheritance: Kinship structures unrecoverable; inferred bilateral or flexible reckoning; inheritance limited to skill transmission and possibly personal tools; no durable property to transmit.

    Civic Association & Self-Organization: Band-level organization only; no guilds, councils, or formal associations; coordination achieved through face-to-face consensus and habitual routine.

    Leisure, Play & Sociability: Inferred from primate parallels; grooming, play, and informal sociability likely central to band cohesion; no recoverable ritual or festive structure.

    Grievance Metabolization & Social Discharge: Fission-fusion band dynamics allow grievance resolution through spatial separation; no formal mediation; unresolved conflict likely triggers group splitting.

    P05. Political Environment

    Power Structure & Authority: No sovereign power; situational leadership based on skill, experience, or kinship seniority; authority diffuse and task-specific; no centralization trend detectable.

    Succession & Power Transfer: No formalized succession; leadership emerges contextually and lapses when task ends; no flashpoint around transfer.

    Legitimacy & Political Narrative: No recoverable legitimacy discourse; compliance sustained through kinship obligation and immediate reciprocity rather than ideological justification.

    Administrative Capacity & Institutional Integrity: No administrative apparatus; coordination achieved through embodied routine and imitative transmission; no records, no taxation, no enforcement machinery.

    Political Participation & Mobilization: All adults participate directly in band decisions through presence and action; no exclusion mechanism; dissent expressed through departure rather than protest.

    Geopolitical Position & External Relations: No recoverable inter-group diplomacy; contact between bands inferred for mating purposes; no alliances, treaties, or territorial boundaries beyond habitual range.

    Coercive Apparatus & Means of Force: No organized coercive capacity; interpersonal violence possible but not institutionalized; weaponry indistinguishable from hunting toolkit.

    Internal Order & Threat Environment: Predation by large carnivores the primary threat; internal disorder limited by small group size and fission option; no banditry or rebellion conceivable at this scale.

    P06. Informational Environment

    Channels & Transmission Velocity: Face-to-face demonstration and imitation the sole channel; information travels at walking pace and no farther than personal contact; no acoustic or visual signaling beyond immediate range.

    Record, Storage & Persistence: Living memory only; no external storage media; knowledge persists solely in the heads and hands of practitioners and is lost when lineages break; lithic reduction sequences encode procedural memory in artifact form but are not retrievable as instruction.

    Framing, Censorship & Discourse Norms: No recoverable framing conventions; pre-linguistic context precludes propositional censorship; norms transmitted through behavioral modeling rather than verbal proscription.

    Reception, Attention & Amplification: Attention constrained to immediate demonstration; no amplification beyond physical proximity; skill acquisition requires sustained observation and practice over years.

    Signal Integrity & Noise Load: High fidelity in face-to-face transmission but cumulative drift possible across generations; no verification mechanism beyond successful replication of technique; noise load low given slow information flow.

    P07. Judicial Environment

    Moral Foundations & Sense of Justice: Inferred baseline reciprocity norms governing sharing and cooperation; no recoverable moral discourse; sense of fairness likely present but pre-propositional.

    Law, Codification & Regulatory Evolution: No formal law or codified rules; conduct governed by habitual practice and immediate social feedback; no regulatory evolution detectable.

    Adjudication, Enforcement & Liability: No formal adjudication; disputes resolved through interpersonal negotiation, avoidance, or group fission; no third-party arbiters, tribunals, or liability assignment mechanisms — informative null for this pre-institutional window.

    Rights, Protections & Compliance Burden: No formalized rights or protections; individual autonomy constrained by kin obligation and cooperative necessity; compliance drag negligible in absence of codified rules.

    Impunity & Unredressed Wrong: No formal justice system to produce impunity gap; wrongs addressed through direct retaliation, reconciliation, or separation; residue accumulates as interpersonal grievance rather than institutional deficit.

    P08. Intellectual Environment

    Inquiry, Theory & Paradigm: No recoverable propositional inquiry; practical problem-solving embedded in tool-making sequences; no theory or paradigm in linguistic sense; causal understanding demonstrated through technical competence.

    Applied Knowledge & Technical Method: Symmetrical handaxe and cleaver production represents sophisticated applied knowledge; mental template, hierarchical reduction sequence, and platform preparation demonstrate engineering capacity transmitted through imitation.

    Education, Knowledge Stock & Skill Distribution: Apprenticeship through observation and guided practice; knowledge stock embodied in skilled practitioners; skill distribution likely concentrated among experienced adults with gradual acquisition by juveniles.

    Obsolescence & Knowledge Decay: Technique vulnerable to lineage extinction; no external record preserves obsolete methods; knowledge decay occurs when transmission chains break during demographic stress or population isolation.

    P09. Cultural Environment

    Mythos, Memory & Foundational Identity: No recoverable mythos or narrative identity; group identity likely grounded in co-residence, shared practice, and kin recognition rather than symbolic origin story.

    Artistic Expression & Material Culture: Symmetrical handaxes exhibit imposed form beyond functional requirement; whether this constitutes aesthetic expression is contested; no unambiguous art or ornamentation attested for this window.

    Collective Mood & Behavioral Disposition: Unrecoverable; inferred stable disposition given continuous occupation and technological refinement; no evidence of crisis or disruption within the trend.

    Form Exhaustion & Meaning Decay: Not applicable in pre-symbolic context; no forms to exhaust or meanings to decay in propositional sense; technique evolves gradually without evidence of stagnation or anomie.

    P10. Spiritual Environment

    Cosmology & Existential Outlook: No recoverable cosmology; whether pre-linguistic hominins possessed existential awareness is contested; no burial or mortuary practice attested for this window.

    Ritual, Devotion & Sacred Practice: No recoverable ritual; repetitive technical sequences may have rhythmic and quasi-ritualized character but lack evidence of sacred intent; informative null.

    Religious Institutions & Doctrinal Authority: No religious institutions; no clerical hierarchy or doctrinal system possible in pre-linguistic context; informative null.

    Spiritual Diffusion, Syncretism & Displacement: Not applicable; no recoverable belief system to diffuse, blend, or displace; category inapplicable to this window.

    Disenchantment & Sacred Discharge: Not applicable; disenchantment presupposes prior enchantment for which no evidence exists; no sacred discharge conceivable in pre-cosmological context.
